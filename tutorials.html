Tutorial Center Services

Mathematics for Kids

Kids Hub offers tutorial services to students who have difficulty in Mathematics. We believe that everybody is capable of learning Mathematics. We will ensure that by the end of the tutorial sessions, the students can solve mathematical problems efficiently.

Reading for Kids

Kids Hub offers tutorial services to students who have difficulty in reading. We believe that everybody should learn how to read and comprehend information. We will ensure that by the end of the tutorial sessions, the students can read properly and interpret information correctly.

Computer Literacy for Kids

Kids Hub offers tutorial services to students who wish to expand their knowledge and improve their skills in computers. We believe that technology has become an essential part of our living. We will ensure that by the end of the tutorial sessions, the students understand computers better and use it effectively in their daily lives.
